# GroupMe

 GroupMe is an app that you can download that will let you message and connect with other people. You can use this app to be in contact with groups of people or you can DM one person. 89% of the respondents know what this term means. 11% of the students at Geneseo do not know what GroupMe is. There was 94% of people who felt that this term did not originate from Geneseo. (Stout Peters, G-M 2025)
