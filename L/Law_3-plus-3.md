# Law 3+3 

This is a program through Geneseo and the University at Buffalo School of Law. This program allows a student to take three years of school at Geneseo and then transfer their credits to the University at Buffalo. 89% of the respondents claimed that they knew the term Law 3+3. 94% of the respondents claimed that this term originated from Geneseo. (Stout Peters, G-M 2025)
