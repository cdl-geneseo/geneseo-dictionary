Tavern: (noun)

1. The bar area of The Big Tree Inn restaurant

2. The notably cheaper menu offered at The Big Tree Inn, usually referred to as the &quot;Tavern menu&quot;

A common term used by Geneseo students and locals to refer to the bar area of The Big Tree Inn, a historic restaurant located just off Main Street and adjacent to the SUNY Geneseo campus. Known for its cozy, wood-paneled atmosphere and casual vibe, the Tavern offered a more affordable alternative to the main dining room, featuring a separate “Tavern menu” with student-friendly prices and pub-style fare. 

In a study done to determine of students know this word, about 75% of knew this word and could define it. About 16.7% were familiar with the word but could not define it. 8.3% of students did not know the word at all.
