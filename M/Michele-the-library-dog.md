# Michele the library dog

n. Michele the library dog is a black labrador retriever and certified therapy dog. She visits Milne library a few times each month, offering support and serving as a friendly face to students at SUNY Geneseo. As of 2025, 100% of surveyed students were familiar with the term. 93% thought that the term had local origins. (Lopez, G-M, 2025)
